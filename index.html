01 — About

Machine learning, built for the real world

I'm a BSc Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning student at ISBAT University in Kampala, learning how to take machine learning beyond the notebook — building APIs, dashboards, and deployments, and figuring out the hard parts as I go.

Lately I've been exploring credit risk and fintech for African lending systems. SACCOs and microfinance institutions serve millions of borrowers with thin credit files, and building scoring tools for that world has taught me more about real-world ML — messy data, explainability, trust — than any course could.

I'm still early in this journey and genuinely enjoy that. Whether it's NLP, automation, or a new framework, I'd rather try, break things, and learn than wait until I feel like an expert.
